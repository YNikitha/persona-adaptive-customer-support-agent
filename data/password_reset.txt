Password Reset Guide

Users can reset their password by clicking the "Forgot Password" link on the login page.

Steps:
1. Enter your registered email address.
2. Check your email for a reset link.
3. Create a new password with at least 8 characters.
4. Use a combination of uppercase letters, lowercase letters, numbers, and symbols.

If the email is not received within 10 minutes, check the spam folder or contact support.
